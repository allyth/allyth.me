About Me

Hi! My name's Ally, I'm 20 years old, and I live in Chicago. Starting 2019, I am an undergraduate student at DePaul University, majoring in Computer Science (concentration in Software Development) with a Data Science minor. As of July 2019, I am studying machine learning, computer vision, and data mining at the Data Science Center of DePaul University and I am a member of the Visual Informatics and Data Analytics Group. Since February 2020, I work as a part-time Undergraduate Research Assistant at DePaul's Data Science Center; in addition, starting October 2020, I am the Student Assistant for the Associate Provost for Research.
